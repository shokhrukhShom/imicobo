# Copyright & Content Policy

**Last updated: July 2026**

This page explains how music, video, and images work in **iMicobo** and the **imicobo.com** website, and how to raise a copyright concern.

---

## We do not host music or video

iMicobo does **not** host, store, upload, or distribute any songs, karaoke tracks, or videos. When you play a song on imicobo.com, it streams through **YouTube’s official embedded player** directly from YouTube. All such content is owned by its respective rights holders and delivered by YouTube, subject to YouTube’s Terms of Service and the uploader’s permissions (which is why some videos can only be played on YouTube directly).

**Album artwork** shown on the site is retrieved from **Apple’s public iTunes Search API** and remains the property of its respective owners. It is used to help you visually identify songs.

iMicobo acts as a way to browse and select this publicly available content and to use your phone as a microphone. It is not a source of the underlying recordings.

## Your voice, your performance

Audio you sing is streamed peer‑to‑peer from your phone to your own screen and is not recorded or stored by us. You are responsible for your own use of iMicobo, including any public performance, recording, or streaming you may choose to do with other tools, and for complying with the rights of the underlying works.

## Copyright concerns / takedown requests

Because we do not host the underlying music or video, most content concerns are best directed to the platform that hosts the file (for example, YouTube). If you believe something presented through iMicobo or imicobo.com infringes your rights and involves material we control, please contact us with:

1. Your name and contact information,
2. Identification of the work you believe is infringed,
3. The specific location (URL or description) of the material in question,
4. A statement of your good‑faith belief that the use is not authorized, and
5. A statement that the information is accurate and that you are the rights holder or authorized to act on their behalf.

**Send copyright notices to:** support@imicobo.com

We will review and respond promptly, including removing or disabling access to material we control where appropriate.
