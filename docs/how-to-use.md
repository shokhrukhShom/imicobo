# How to Use iMicobo

**Sing your favorite karaoke online — your iPhone becomes the microphone for any screen.**

iMicobo turns your iPhone into a wireless microphone. You pick songs on a big screen (your computer, laptop, or smart TV browser) at **imicobo.com**, and sing into your phone — your voice comes out of the screen’s speakers, right over the music.

---

## What you need

1. **This app** on your iPhone.
2. **A screen with a web browser** on the same Wi‑Fi — a laptop, desktop, or a smart TV that can open a website. This is your “karaoke screen.”
3. Both on the **same Wi‑Fi network**.

---

## Step 1 — Open the karaoke screen

On your computer or TV, open a web browser and go to:

**https://imicobo.com**

You’ll see the song library. This is the screen everyone will watch and hear.

## Step 2 — Start pairing

On imicobo.com, click **“📱 Pair iPhone”** (top of the page). A **QR code** and a **4‑digit code** will appear.

## Step 3 — Connect your phone

Open iMicobo on your iPhone and either:

- **Scan the QR code** — tap *Scan the QR code* and point your camera at the screen, **or**
- **Enter the code** — tap *Enter code instead* and type the 4 digits shown on the screen.

That’s it — in a second or two the screen shows the microphone is live, and you’ll see the connection quality at the bottom.

## Step 4 — Pick a song and sing

On the screen, browse the rows (Editor’s Choice, Pop, Rock, and more) or use the **search bar** at the top to find any song. Click a song — the karaoke video plays full‑screen with the words on screen. Sing into your phone. 🎤

---

## Choosing songs

- **Browse** the rows: Editor’s Choice, Pop, Rock, Alternative Rock, Retro 80s, Oldies, R&B & Soul, International, and Popular Meme Songs.
- **Search** for anything using the bar at the top.
- **Recently sung** keeps your last songs handy at the top.

## Sound effects

Tap the **sliders icon** in the app to open effects: reverb, echo, tone, and one‑tap presets (Clean, Karaoke, Speech, Big Room). Effects are applied on the screen, so they add almost no delay to your voice.

## Muting

- Tap the big **mic button** in the app to mute/unmute.
- You can also **mute from your iPhone’s lock screen or Control Center** — the play/pause control toggles your mic (Pause = muted, Play = live), so you don’t need to unlock your phone.

## Reconnecting

Your screen keeps the **same code** even if you refresh or reopen the page, and your phone remembers the last code — so getting back in is quick.

---

## Tips for the best sound (lowest delay)

Your voice travels over Wi‑Fi, so the network matters:

- **Wire the screen if you can.** Connecting your computer/TV to the router with an Ethernet cable (or a cheap USB‑C→Ethernet adapter or powerline adapter) gives the biggest improvement — it removes a wireless hop.
- **Use the 5 GHz Wi‑Fi band** and stay close to the router.
- **Turn on WMM/Wi‑Fi QoS** in your router settings (usually on by default) — iMicobo tags your mic as priority voice traffic so a good router gives it a faster lane.
- **A wired or plug‑in microphone** into the computer also works with zero lag, if you prefer.

---

## Troubleshooting

**“Can’t reach the screen.”** Make sure the karaoke screen is open at imicobo.com and your phone has internet. Try the QR scan, or re‑enter the code.

**Noticeable delay.** Almost always the Wi‑Fi. Move closer to the router, switch to 5 GHz, or wire the screen with Ethernet. A quick mute/unmute also resets any built‑up delay instantly.

**A song opens YouTube instead of playing on the screen.** A few videos have embedding disabled by their uploader, so they can only play on YouTube directly. Most songs play right on the screen.

**Camera won’t scan.** Make sure you allowed camera access, or just use *Enter code instead*.

**Voice keeps running with the screen off.** That’s intended — iMicobo keeps the mic live when your phone locks so you can keep singing.
