# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how the **iMicobo** app (“iMicobo,” “we,” “us”) and the **imicobo.com** website handle information. iMicobo is a wireless‑microphone app: your iPhone acts as a microphone for a karaoke screen you open in a web browser.

Our approach is simple: **we collect as little as possible, and your voice is never sent to or stored on our servers.**

---

## The short version

- **Your voice/audio is streamed directly (peer‑to‑peer) from your phone to your own screen. It does not pass through our servers and is never recorded or stored.**
- The **camera** is used only to scan the on‑screen pairing QR code, on your device. No photos or video are taken, transmitted, or stored.
- The **app does not require an account**, does not ask for your name, email, or contacts, and contains **no advertising or third‑party tracking SDKs**.
- The **imicobo.com website** uses Google Analytics to understand general, aggregate usage.

---

## Information the iPhone app handles

**Microphone audio.** When you connect, your microphone audio is sent over a direct, encrypted peer‑to‑peer connection (WebRTC) to the karaoke screen you paired with. It is used only to play your voice live on that screen. **We do not receive, record, store, or have access to this audio.**

**Camera.** Used solely to scan the pairing QR code shown on your karaoke screen. Image processing happens on your device in real time; nothing is captured or sent anywhere.

**Local network / connection.** To connect your phone and screen, a small amount of technical “signaling” data (a pairing code and connection details) passes briefly through our relay server so the two devices can find each other. This is a few hundred bytes of setup information — not your audio — and pairing sessions expire automatically.

**On‑device settings.** The app stores small preferences on your phone only — such as your effect settings and the last pairing code — so reconnecting is quick. This never leaves your device and is removed if you delete the app.

The app itself contains **no analytics, advertising, or tracking**.

---

## Information the imicobo.com website handles

The karaoke screen runs at imicobo.com. When you use it:

- **Analytics.** We use **Google Analytics** to measure aggregate, non‑identifying usage (for example, how many people visit and which features are used). This uses cookies. You can block cookies in your browser or use a tracker‑blocking extension.
- **Song lookups.** When you play or search for a song, the site asks our server to find the matching video; results are cached so popular songs are served quickly. Album artwork is fetched from Apple’s public iTunes Search API.
- **Basic server logs.** Like most websites, our server may briefly log standard request information (such as IP address and request time) for security and reliability. We do not use these to identify individuals.

---

## Third‑party services

iMicobo relies on a few third‑party services to work. Their handling of data is governed by their own privacy policies:

- **YouTube / Google** — karaoke videos play through YouTube’s official embedded player on the website.
- **Google Analytics** — website usage analytics.
- **Apple iTunes Search API** — album cover images shown on the website.
- **Google STUN server** — helps your phone and screen discover each other for the direct connection (exchanges network address information only).

## Children’s privacy

iMicobo is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app requires no account and collects no personal data, it does not build profiles of any user, including children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will address it.

---

## Data retention

- **Audio:** never stored (it is peer‑to‑peer and transient).
- **Pairing sessions:** temporary; they expire automatically after inactivity.
- **Song/artwork cache:** stored on our server to speed up lookups; contains song titles and public media references, not personal information.
- **On‑device settings:** remain on your phone until you delete the app.

## Security

Connections to imicobo.com use HTTPS, and the phone‑to‑screen audio link is encrypted (a requirement of WebRTC). No system is perfectly secure, but because we don’t collect or store personal data or your audio, there is very little about you for us to expose.

## Your choices

- Decline or revoke **microphone** or **camera** permissions in iOS Settings (the app won’t function as a mic without microphone access).
- Block cookies or use a tracker blocker to limit website analytics.
- Delete the app to remove all on‑device settings.
