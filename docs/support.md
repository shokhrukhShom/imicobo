# iMicobo Support

Need a hand? Most questions are answered below. If you’re still stuck, email us and we’ll help.

**Contact:** support@imicobo.com
**Typical response time:** within 1–2 business days.

---

## Quick start

1. On a computer or smart‑TV browser, open **https://imicobo.com**.
2. Click **“📱 Pair iPhone”** to show a QR code and a 4‑digit code.
3. In the iMicobo app, **scan the QR** or **enter the code**.
4. Pick a song on the screen and sing into your phone.

A full walkthrough is in our **How to Use** guide.

## Frequently asked questions

**Do I need an account?**
No. iMicobo needs no sign‑up, email, or password.

**Is it free?**
Yes, iMicobo is free to use.

**My phone says “Can’t reach the screen.”**
Make sure the karaoke screen is open at **imicobo.com** and your phone has an internet connection. Then scan the QR code again or re‑enter the 4‑digit code. The code on the screen stays the same even if you refresh.

**There’s a delay/echo in my voice.**
This is almost always your Wi‑Fi. For the best results: connect the computer/TV to your router with **Ethernet** (even a cheap USB‑C→Ethernet or powerline adapter helps a lot), use the **5 GHz** Wi‑Fi band, and stay close to the router. A quick **mute then unmute** also instantly clears any built‑up delay.

**A song opened YouTube instead of playing on the screen.**
Some videos have embedding turned off by their uploader and can only play on YouTube directly. Most songs play right on the karaoke screen.

**Can I use effects (reverb, echo)?**
Yes — tap the sliders icon in the app for presets and manual controls. Effects are applied on the screen, so they add almost no delay.

**Can I mute without unlocking my phone?**
Yes. Use the play/pause control on your **lock screen or Control Center** — Pause mutes your mic, Play unmutes it.

**Does it keep working with my screen off?**
Yes, iMicobo keeps your mic live when the phone locks so you can keep singing.

**The camera won’t scan the code.**
Check that you allowed camera access for iMicobo in iOS Settings, or just tap **“Enter code instead.”**

**Which devices can be the “screen”?**
Any device with a modern web browser on the same network — laptop, desktop, or a smart TV that can open a website.
