Connect a microphone

Two ways to sing — pick whichever you have.

Option A · Use your iPhone as the mic (Wi-Fi)

  1. Keep this screen open on your computer or TV.
  2. Put your iPhone and this computer on the same Wi-Fi — ideally the 5 GHz band (often named “…-5G”). It has far less lag than 2.4 GHz.
  3. Open the pairing screen, then scan the QR code or type the 4-digit code on your phone.
  4. Sing — your voice comes out of this screen’s speakers, over the music.
Open the iPhone pairing screen →

Option B · Plug in a microphone (no lag at all)

  1. Plug a USB or 3.5 mm mic into this computer.
  2. Set it as the input in your system sound settings, with “listen/monitor” on so you hear yourself.
  3. Hit play on any song and go.

Tip: if your voice sounds delayed, it’s almost always the Wi-Fi — move closer to the router or switch to the 5 GHz network.
